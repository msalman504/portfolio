DROPSHIPPING
& E-COMMERCE

E-Commerce · Dropshipping · Remote

Ran paid advertising for dropshipping and e-commerce stores — products shipped directly to customers with no physical inventory. The challenge was high CPAs from untargeted spend and no real conversion system in place. I built the full funnel from scratch using Meta Ads and WhatsApp, applying behavioral psychology at every touchpoint to drive purchases.

Built end-to-end acquisition funnel: Meta Ads to capture buyer intent, WhatsApp as the frictionless channel to close sales and handle objections.
Precise audience targeting: Lookalike audiences built from actual buyers, layered with purchase-behavior interest stacks to minimize wasted spend.
Systematic A/B testing: one variable changed per test, winning creatives scaled within 72 hours — responsible for a 40% CTR improvement.
Scripted WhatsApp sales sequences using behavioral psychology: reciprocity triggers, urgency framing, and conversational closers to push undecided buyers over the line.
